Character

Description

Collins Dictionary on its 6th line defines the character as having "the ability to deal effectively with difficult, unpleasant, or dangerous situations", don't believe me?, see for yourself Collins dictionary - character If you ever wanted to get the character yourself, here is your chance, this is the only time you will be able to get the character by following this simple recipe below. Please ensure that all of the supplemental ingredients are added to the main recipe to avoid complete failure in life.

Ingredients

  • Moving before 18 - at least 3 times
  • Moving to another country w/out anyone at 17 - 1 time
  • Experience culture shock while no one understands you - 1.5 years
  • Face exhaustion of funds 2 month before the next "care package" - 1 time
  • Overcoming language barrier while maintaining the grades - 4 semesters
  • Losing one of your parents and not being able to attend the funeral - 1 time
  • Irresponsible spending that leads to absence of funds for tuition - 1 time
  • Miscellaneous troubles (fights, arguments, racism, harsh treatment, temptations) - constant

Supplemental Ingredients

  • Strict and just teacher - at least 1
  • Supportive teacher - at least 2
  • Girlfriend, to tell you "If you won't straighten up, I will leave" - at least 1 of them
  • Person willing to help financially, who eventually becomes best friend - at least 1
  • Friends for emotional support - constant

Steps

  1. First and foremost take 3 times of moving and let it sit before you are completely situated
  2. Once you see that moving 3 times sits well with you, quickly add moving to another country, all the while you only turned 17
  3. As soon as you have the mix ready, add to it culture shock, exhaustion of funds, and language barrier
  4. At this time it is strongly advised to add strict and just teacher, it won't relief the bitterness of the mix, but will serve as agent of stability, also add some friends
  5. Once you have, more or less, stabilized the mix dump into it losing one of your parents
  6. Here, once again supplemental ingredient is necessary for emotional support, I will let you guess which one
  7. Hopefully you have been pouring miscellaneous troubles all along from the beginning, if not please dump amount equal to a mass that you have prepared so far
  8. Finally add financial irresponsibility and to avoid completely disastrous mix add a person with a willingness to help
  9. Top it all off with more miscellaneous troubles and emotionally supportive friends

Voila! The character has been formed!
